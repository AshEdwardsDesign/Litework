FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How effective is the treatment?

The treatment is very effective. Most customers experience results up to 12 shades whiter.

How long will my teeth remain white?

This depends on your diet; if you drink red wine and smoke then a 15 minute topup every 3 months is reccomended. Having a topup every 3 to 4 months will keep your teeth nice and white as stains do build up naturally.

Are there any special requirements post treatment?

It is highly advisable that for 24 hours post-treatment you avoid any staining food or drink. A diet of water and chicken/rice/pasta and other white foods is preferable. We will provide details of suitable food and drink at your treatment.

In order to maintain tooth whiteness for as long as possible, staining food and drink (such as cola or wine) and smoking should be reduced to a minimum.

Is teeth whitening by a non-dentist legal?

Yes, teeth whitening by a non-dentist is perfectly legal provided the person is qualified and insured. Therefore Service Your Smile are legally allowed to carry out treatments.
