Are there any side effects?

The “cold” laser does not cause any tissue damage as a healthy cell will exhibit weak or no effects from the low-power laser while unhealthy cells will exhibit strong effects. The photons of the laser are strong enough to alter the shape of certain receptor proteins in the mitochondrial cell membrane, but weak enough not to damage crucial molecules such as DNA, enzymes (proteins), and lipids.
