Tattoo Removal Norwich

We are priced to provide affordable treatments and offer great value.

Your treatment will start with a free 20 minute consultation to assess your skin and tattoo to determine which treatment will be best suited to you and your needs. We will also talk to you about the way in which you can ensure that your treatment is comfortable and provide you with information about aftercare.

We will use the laser on a very small part of your tattoo to ensure that you do not have an abnormal reaction to the treatment or to the breakdown of the ink. And the good news is that we can begin your treatment from as little as a week after your patch test.
