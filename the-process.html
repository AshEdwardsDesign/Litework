The Process

Here you can discover how teeth whitening works and why our treatment is the most effective

We offer several treatments so we can fit around you

Step One

In order to start the treatment we will take your teeth shade so that the difference your treatment has made can be measured. You will also need to fill out our consent form which will allow us to carry out the whitening of your teeth.

Step Two

You will be taken to one of our comfy beds where you will open your prefilled mouthtray, place on your protective goggles and place the tray into your mouth. One of our clinic staff will then turn on the LED light which activates the gel in your mouthtray. The activated gel will proceed to break down the stains on your teeth. Once the 20 minutes is complete, you will remove the tray and rinse off the gel accompanied by one of our staff. This is repeated dependant on the package you have chosen.

Step Three

Once your treatment(s) are complete, we will retest the shade of your teeth and show you the difference!
