The Treatment

Ultrasound cavitation uses low frequency soundwaves, causing the fat cells in a targeted area to vibrate at great speed causing friction and therefore heat. This heat transforms the fat from a solid state to a liquid, allowing it to be drained from your body via the lymphatic system.

In other words, this technology melts your fat and then you flush it from your body when you sweat or visit the loo!

Although, each treatment will permanently destroy fat cells, it is important to remember that it cannot prevent your body from creating new ones. Therefore, a healthy and balanced diet and regular exercise are essential if you want to see results. Ultrasound cavitation is an aid to reducing fat, not a magic wand.

It's also really important that you keep yourself well hydrated throughout the treatment period in order to flush the toxins from your body.
