What can Premier Cars Offer you?

Premier Cars will make sure you succeed, provided you are highly motivated.

Premier Cars can provide:-

  • Professional business set up, training and guidance
  • Significant new business opportunities
  • Marketing support
  • Advice on preferred vehicle deals

Training and Support

Induction and Training

Premier Cars can provide a training programme for franchisees to be able to operate the business. This covers advice on how to set up your own business, customer relation skills, operating the business, and accountancy/book-keeping on a one to one basis.

Mentoring

We provide assistance to franchisees with on the ground mentoring and practical support, to help you through the critical first months of running your own business. This forms the second phase of your training through “on the job” experience.

Ongoing Coaching

In addition to the practical skills learned through coaching and training, you will have telephone coaching each month, if you wish. This is there to assist you setting your own goals and helping you achieve them.

Back Office Support

We want to do everything we can to provide the support you need to stay focused on servicing your clients. We can offer you regular advice on all aspects of the day to day client booking system and how to achieve a good customer base.

Franchising as a business model

Latest Positive Figures For Franchise Businesses From NatWest Franchise Survey

While other sectors have struggled in recent years, the NatWest BFA Franchise survey records some positive news. Growth, job creation and exporting are all mentioned! While UK GDP grew by 0.7% in 2011, the franchise industry increased economic contribution by 8% as turnover rose to £13.4 billion.

9 out of 10 franchise businesses posted a profit over the year – reducing loss-makers to pre-recession levels. Further growth is likely, as 76% of those surveyed expect business to improve in the coming months.

80% of franchisees believe that franchising gives them a competitive advantage over other small businesses, as they have the support of a larger brand.

Source: NatWest BFA Franchise Survey.

The Story of Premier Cars

It took months of planning - quite a daunting task to set up your own business and to get it running smoothly. Two years later my wife joined me in the business and it went from strength to strength with top quality cars and drivers.
