BUSINESS DETAILS

If you have you always had a desire to work for yourself, we can offer you the opportunity to follow our tried and tested business model and start your own leaflet distribution business with our full training and continued support giving you the potential to earn between £40k and £80k per annum within your first 2 years trading.

The business provides customers with a localised direct mail service utilising the Royal mail to deliver leaflets for its customers which in some cases have generated more business than they have experienced from any other form of advertising media!

Some of the regular customers who have taken advantage of this unique service include: Pizza Hut, Subway, Cartridge World, Chips Away, De Vere Hotel Group, Dominos, and many, many other localised businesses which are found in the majority of areas throughout the UK.

To purchase a Franchise in your area or the established franchise in Yorkshire which is currently available the cost is £6,250 followed by a Royalty payment which is 6% of annual turnover.

Your Franchise will include the following: –

  • An exclusive territory within the UK (North West & Yorkshire currently available)Submit
  • A comprehensive training programme & insight into the company framework
  • Continued guidance and support from head office and other franchisees
  • An experienced team to help with artwork/design re customer requirements

To take advantage of this unique opportunity you’ll need: –

  • Enthusiasm, passion and a genuine love for meeting new people
  • Drive, energy and a smile which are an advantage to any business
  • Sales & Customer service experience not essential but would be an advantage
  • Good organisation skills and self discipline in order to provide a reliable service
