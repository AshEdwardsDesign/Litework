Pitman shorthand has revolutionized office communications since 1837 and today the revolutionary spirit lives on.

A Business Superbrand*, Pitman Training provides exceptional centre-based office and IT training for thousands of people every year throughout the UK and overseas.

Quality is at the heart of the Pitman Training experience and the strength of the Pitman brand is unrivalled in the sector. We also truly meet customer needs, which gives us a genuine competitive advantage. It's why we've grown during the recession and why we're searching for more talented and ambitious franchise owners.

We call our offer The Pitman Advantage and if you've got the right blend of skills, drive and determination we'd love to hear from you.

* Daily Telegraph Business Superbrands 2009/2010

Why Pitman Training?

If you are a people-person as well as business person, owning and running a Pitman Training centre could give you a satisfying and rewarding life.

Running your own business is always challenging, but over the past 17 years, we've developed a winning formula comprising the following:

  • Brand leader in our market
  • Financial Stability - growth through the recession, within a franchise established for 19 years
  • Proven business model – 100+ franchises in the UK and worldwide
  • Unique offer - flexible, in tune with today's needs
  • Growing market - IT is fast moving, creating continuous demand multi-sector - private consumers, business and public sector sophisticated business management systems and marketing
  • Support bespoke products that lead to recognised qualifications

Financial Information

The investment and returns available to Pitman Training franchise owners vary on a number of key factors – most of which are firmly within your control.

Your business will be what you make it, with our back up and support of course.

  • Franchise fee from £22,500 + VAT
  • Typical start up costs from £65,000 + VAT
  • Typical third year sales of £165,000

Franchise agreements run for five years. Renewal rates are high with few of our franchises wanting to resell.
