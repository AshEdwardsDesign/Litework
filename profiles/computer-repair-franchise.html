BUSINESS DETAILS

The PC PAL Franchise is the UKs leading provider of PC Repair and Support Franchises, providing on-site computer services to homes and small businesses.

We offer the most comprehensive, proven and best-supported opportunity to start your own profitable business in the rapidly growing and lucrative PC Repair and Support markets.

PC PAL are looking for hardworking and ambitious people to join our franchise network, helping to serve the thousands of customers who are already choosing PC PAL to solve their PC problems!

Would it Suit Me?

We are looking for:

  • Computer enthusiasts who are looking to turn their hobby into a profitable business and a good income.
  • Existing computer engineers who are fed up with working for somebody else and want to earn real money for themselves. (And, if you are already suitably qualified, we will even reduce your joining fee!)
  • Managers who would like to build their own business in the highly lucrative home computing sector – even if they have no experience of computers themselves.

We will make every effort to help you to make a properly informed decision on whether or not this exciting business is right for you.

The Franchise Package

We’ve developed an unbeatable turnkey package to allow you to hit the ground running and which costs £19,999+VAT for a Mobile Engineer.

This includes:

  • Exclusive marketing rights in your chosen territory of 180,000 population.
  • A comprehensive PC Repair tool-kit including laptop, software, diagnostic tools and initial stock of parts.
  • A marketing and launch plan tailored to your area - we don’t have a ‘one-size-fits-all’ approach like some franchises.
  • Your first directory adverts for Yellow Pages, The BT Phonebook and Thomson Local.
  • Our unrivalled step-by-step training and support package with on-site experience included.
  • Dedicated and comprehensive online knowledge-base built from 1000s of hours of real-life, on-site problem solving.
  • Technical support over the phone and online whenever you need it, so you never feel on your own.

Hub Managers can bolt-on additional territories for just £10,000 + VAT, giving exclusive marketing rights in a further 180,000 population. 'Marketing' and 'Engineer' bolt-ons can then be purchased as and when each area comes on stream, thereby improving cash-flow during the expansion phase.

Training and Support

PC PAL have partnered with the UK's leading provider of IT training courses and services, to carry out the internationally recognised CompTIA A+ and Microsoft Certified Desktop Support Technician (MCDST) training.

Our own PC PAL System Training Course is held at our Head Office where you will learn all you need to know to get your business up and running successfully. Our unique PC PAL Repair System is all about putting the classroom training into context in the real world. You won’t find this information anywhere else!
