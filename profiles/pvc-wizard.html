BUSINESS DETAILS

This is a fantastic opportunity to run your own Conservatory and PVC cleaning company providing you with a designated and exclusive territory within the North West, where you will be encouraged to build up your own network of clients through a mix of the guidance and specialist marketing provided as well as having the freedom to implement your own ideas of sourcing new customers.

Franchise opportunities are an excellent way of becoming your own boss, giving you the freedom to work flexible hours with the added benefit of knowing the harder you work the bigger the financial reward.

This exclusive franchise is currently being offered to interested parties for an initial £15,000 followed by an annual royalty payment which is 10% of annual turnover with a further 2.5% commitment to marketing.

Your Franchise will include the following:–

  • An Exclusive Territory within the North West
  • An “everything you need to know” Training Programme
  • Continued Support from head office and other franchisees
  • Easy to follow manuals and continued support
  • All equipment needed to provide the full range of services offered

To take advantage of this unique opportunity you’ll need:–

  • Enthusiasm, passion and a genuine liking for the outdoors
  • Drive, energy and a smile which are an advantage to any business
  • Sales/Customer service experience not essential but would be an advantage
  • Good organisation skills and self discipline in order to provide a reliable service
