A fresh, new you

Beloved by “A listers” and now affordable for everyone this revolutionary laser treatment gives your skin a new burst of life, restoring that youthful glow.

Carbon Laser Rejuvenation is a completely painless and non-invasive treatment which focuses on stimulating natural elastin and boosting collagen production for more refreshed and glowing skin. It exfoliates to minimising pores and reduce the appearance of wrinkles. Skin tone is improved and more even as blemishes and acne scars are reduced, to reveal a new, more radiant you.

How does it work?

After we have talked you through your treatment, we will cleanse the skin to remove any makeup and provide the optimum base for the treatment. We will then apply a thin layer of carbon paste onto your skin, which is very quickly absorbed into your pores.

We do this because when a thin layer of carbon is spread over the skin the laser is absorbed by the carbon, focusing on the epidermis layer. Once the treatment is complete, the skin is cleansed, toned and treated with a serum that is rich in Vitamin C.
