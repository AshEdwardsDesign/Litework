The Product

Mobile phone charging vending is currently very successful in Asia and the United States. Now it’s proving to do the same in the UK & Ireland. Our advertising screens are a different and unique concept in comparison to your “standard” vending machines. What makes these machines unique when it comes to vending is; “you can get involved”. This enables you to generate a serious income and you do not require hundreds of machines to do it. We currently have operators with only 5 machines making £3,000.00 per month & more!

“If they can do it, so can you with our help”

They’ll charge 95% of all mobile phones. The only phones they won’t charge are the older models that are no longer common. Simply plug in your phone, put £1 in and you’re away! Customers can use their phones immediately if they have an emergency call/txt to make.

Whilst their phone is charging, they will be looking at very lucrative advertisements that are on your machines. Creating a captive audience in-turn helps to sell advertising as local businesses can see the benefits of a low-cost, unique advertising method that reaches out to huge audiences.

The Opportunity

Mobile Phone Charging Stations are located and installed by us into venues in your chosen area. Everyone has run out of charge at some point or another whilst out and about. Imagine having 5, 10 or 20 of these stations in and around your local area, all collecting £1 per charge every time someone needs to use them. Not only will these machines offer you the traditional ease of collecting your vending revenue, but with the addition of digital advertising screens, this is a unique and very profitable way of increasing your revenue.

Local businesses will pay you to advertise on your stations, usually £5, £10, £20 per month, or more, dependent on how many stations you have. It’s so cheap to advertise on these stations compared to, say, your local newspapers’ classified ads, so you’ll easily sell your advertising space and local businesses will have a cheap form of advertising that literally thousands of potential customers will view each week.

The adverts rotate on a slideshow 24/7, every minute of the day or for as long as the venue is open for. Also, understand the additional benefit of having your own advertising business, especially if you have other businesses. If you feel like you wish to operate your own business, with very little effort, but for maximum rewards; then this opportunity is for you.

Readymade Business will ensure that your products are marketed to the right venues in your chosen area. In order to ensure the right locations are obtained we will offer the venue 20% of the COIN revenue only. For a prime location to be interested in hosting one of your machines it is imperative they get something in return. Ultimately they are offering an extra service to their customers which is of high benefit but 20% of the coin revenue would be the deal breaker. In the illustrative figures below we reflect those calculations.

Please Note: The commission payable to the venue is based on the COIN revenue and NOT the advertising revenue.

FAQ

Question:

Where have these machines been stationed in the UK?

Answer:

We currently have hundreds across the UK & Ireland in pubs, arena’s, airports, cafe’s, hotels, shopping centres and more! Call us to see if your area is available.

Question:

You state: "Full training & on-going support" - How long is the support offered for?

Answer:

For as long as is needed. We wish you to purchase more machines in the future, so it is in our interest to help you all the way to achieve this. Having said that, this business is very straight forward and most people tend not to need us after a few weeks, but we are always there for our operators.

Question:

If the vending machine stopped working through a fault or suffered vandalism, how long does it take to send an engineer out and/or replace with a new machine?

Answer:

We can send out a new machine almost immediately. Our engineer can fix most problems over the phone with you, if not, the callout can take a few days, depending on how busy we are. We recommend all operators carry a spare machine to avoid any interruption, having said that, these machines are very reliable and almost never go wrong, unless vandalised - which is also very rare.

Question:

How does the advertiser supply an advert to place on the machine?

Answer:

Adverts are supplied via email. Most companies will already have an advert ready to go in JPEG format. You simply download this to a USB stick and upload it to the machine, very easy and full training is given. It’s just like using your home DVD player.

Question:

How long does finding advertisers and the locations take?

Answer:

Subject to the size of your order, on average around 6 to 8 weeks, mainly to secure good venues for your machines and not to rush into the first ones that come along. Please note that the advertisers we acquire have agreed to a 1 month free trial of their advert. After 1 month you should contact them to see if they wish to continue on a paid-for basis.

Question:

You mentioned: "Ongoing commission payments of up to £1,000 from advertising our other business on your machines" - How many businesses and how long is each advert shown for?

Answer:

We currently run over 18 opportunities, if you allow us to place these on your machines (optional) we will pay you up to £1,000.00 in commission for any sold from your machines. Your 0843 number will be placed on the machines, so they would call you directly, you’ll then be able to keep track of our inquiries. You pass on the inquiry to us, we do everything else and if they sign up, we pay you. Easy money for doing nothing. You can set your adverts to run from 2 seconds to 10 seconds each.

Question:

Can you order just one vending machine?

Answer:

No, 2 is the minimum order. This business works on numbers. Ideally, you’ll need to purchase 5+ machines. This will give you the best possible returns from the beginning. Especially with the advertising side, as feedback suggests, advertisers like more machines to get value for money and this also makes selling the ad space easier.

Question:

Can they recharge iphones and ipads?

Answer:

Yes, including all other popular makes and models, e.g. Samsung Galaxy, HTC, Blackberry etc.

Question:

Can I speak to one of your operators?

Answer:

Yes, and you may also visit us for a full demonstration here at our office in Cheshire.

Question:

What do I do now if I want to go ahead?

Answer:

Simply call us on 01625 668 630 to see if your area is available, if it is, we can take a £500+VAT deposit from you. We will then send out the appropriate paperwork, which you complete and return. Full payment is required before your machines are installed.
