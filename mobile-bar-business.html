THE PRODUCT

Our Hire Bar business is a fantastic and very profitable business that can be run in your spare time, and simply put, can make you thousands of pounds’ profit in just one night. You can operate on a more management basis or, attend the venues and work behind the bar if you wish.

From birthday parties to weddings or private functions, there is literally, never ending, repeat business, waiting for you to turn up and serve them great beer, drinks, snacks or, even food. Following our proven system and training (no experience required) you’ll be able to operate your own outside bar/catering with ease!

Your main tasks are to organise the hiring out of the equipment to the customer for them to use themselves and supplying them with beer, drinks, etc. Or, supplying the equipment, drinks, food and staff to run the function yourself, which is the most popular and most lucrative side of the business.

There are no overheads, as you can operate from home and you won’t need much space. A small spare room, garage or shed is sufficient.
