Our laser

Fresh Laser Clinics only use state of the art Q-Switch Lasers, which are the industry’s leading tattoo removal lasers because they offer extremely short pulses of light to destroy tattoo pigment. Many other operators use other lasers, including IPL lasers, that produce a much longer light pulse. This merely heats the pigment and can lead to painful scarring.

Along with the gold standard of lasers, we offer a free consultation and patch test so that we can discuss your treatment plan fully, including some options that will make the removal process more comfortable for you.
