THE PRODUCT

We are the sole importers and suppliers of this revolutionary, patented pic ‘n’ mix system, which is currently taking Europe by storm. Sales have increased by up to 50% since being introduced and we have just launched it here for the UK & Ireland market. Our Mixmo module systems are the latest in hygienic, multi sweet dispensing units. There is nothing like our system and these machines are replacing the conventional, current, un-hygienic open hatch systems. They dispense dozens of your favourite pic ‘n’ mix sweets in a convenient, hygienic way! This happens at the touch of a button from sealed compartments which no human hands can touch, or contaminate, the sweets inside.

We all love pic ‘n’ mix sweets, but we are put off by the fact that many hands have touched the sweets using the conventional systems that are available at the moment. The hatches open and the sweets are there to be handled in any way people see fit. People handle them, put them back, drop them and pick them up off the floor, etc! There are more germs on the average hand from day to day living, than on a toilet seat...FACT! The hatches are constantly opened, so this leaves the sweets vulnerable to airborne germs from coughs, colds and any other germs floating around, ready for you to come along and help yourself to these potentially contaminated sweets.

NOT ANYMORE!

Our system prevents this, as our compartments are virtually airtight and NO hands can contaminate the sweets inside. You simply hold the button for the sweet you require and it will dispense into the cup without ever being touched by human hands.
