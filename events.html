Events

The Puss in Boots has fantastic nights and events throughout the week, ranging from food nights to games and entertainment for all the family!

Whether you're looking for somewhere to bring your family for a weekend lunch or dinner, or a great night out for you and your friends, give The Puss in Boots a try!

We now have two systems at The Puss and so can show a wide range of events. Want to watch a particular event/game? Let us know and we can sort it out for you!

We'd love to hear your suggestions and comments regarding our entertainment nights so please don't hesitate to send them in via Facebook or Twitter (using the links at the bottom of this page) or send an email to puss@thepussinboots.co.uk
